# Black Swan Analysis Prompt Template

URGENT — Analyze this unexpected high-impact event:

**Event:** {EVENT_DESCRIPTION}
**Magnitude:** {SEVERITY} (moderate / severe / catastrophic)
**Affected Markets:** {MARKETS}

Current portfolio: {PORTFOLIO_SUMMARY}
Current regime: {REGIME}
Exposure ceiling: {EXPOSURE_CEILING}%

Evaluate:
1. Immediate risk to current positions?
2. Should we reduce exposure? By how much?
3. Are there asymmetric opportunities created by the event?
4. Historical analogues and their resolution timelines?
5. Second-order effects to watch for?

Be conservative. Suggest overrides (-20 to +20) with emphasis on capital preservation.
